Making a Google Account

Even though it's not standard city procedure, it's important for you to have a GMail account to use to work on the spreadsheets in Google Drive.

Multiple department coordinators will be able to collaborate on a spreadsheet in real time which will allow us to move with greater agility and to minimize duplication of effort.

We are asking that you create a city specific Google Account to be able to do work on the inventory.

In order to register for the account, please go to http://bit.ly/gdrivereg and follow the steps to fill out the form and sign up.

Please follow the naming convention of "sdcity" + your first initial + your last name @gmail.com.

For example:

Maksim Pecherskiy (me) would register as “sdcityMPecherskiy@gmail.com”

Once you are finished, please submit your newly created GMail Address at http://bit.ly/gmailregged

Maksim will keep the registry of gmail accounts. Activity on these accounts is subject to the Public Records Act, including any applicable exemptions.

If you have any questions, please email maksimp@sandiego.gov
